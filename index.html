iOS Users: Getting Myntra Product URL

When using Myntra on iPhone, the standard "Share" button copies product details instead of the URL. Follow these steps:

  1. Tap the Share button in Myntra app
  2. Scroll down and select "Copy Link" (not the product name)
  3. Paste the copied link in the field above

Alternative method: Open the product in Safari browser and copy the URL from the address bar.
